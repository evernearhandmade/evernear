## Tips for Faster Entry

- You can duplicate a similar product and just change the name/description/images/price
  instead of starting from scratch each time
- For variants (sizes), on the product page scroll down to "Options" and click
  "Add options like size or color", name the option "Size", then add the values
  (e.g., "Le Classique", "Le Voyage") — Shopify will create the variant rows
  where you can set individual prices
- Upload all images at once by dragging them into the images area
- The product photos are in the img/ folder on your Desktop/devin/ directory
